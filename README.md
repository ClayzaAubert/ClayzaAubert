## 🌙 About Me

I'm just a **wanderer in the world of programming**.

I don't really follow one strict path.  
Sometimes I build APIs, sometimes bots, sometimes websites, sometimes scraping tools, sometimes Android APKs, and sometimes just random things that look fun to try.

For me, coding is not about pretending to know everything.  
It's simply a hobby — a place to explore, break things, fix them, and learn something new along the way.
